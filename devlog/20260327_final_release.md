# Pocket Kingdom v1.0.0 — Final Release Notes
- Date: 2026-03-27
- 47+ autonomous development cycles

## Game Overview
타임어택 탐험 RPG. 마족이 지배하는 세계에서 마석 계약자가 되어 8명의 수호자를 만나고 마왕을 쓰러뜨리는 이야기.

## Final Statistics
- 35 JS modules, ~14,000 lines of code
- 216KB JS bundle (64KB gzipped)
- 31 tilemaps covering entire world
- 102 monsters, 346 skills, 42 items
- 117 NPCs, 50 hidden events, 11 zone bosses
- 15 achievements, 3 difficulty levels, 3 endings
- Full mobile touch support, PWA ready

## Key Features
### Core Systems
- Time-attack expedition (240AP budget)
- Day/night cycle with magi density
- Camping system (3 tiers + food + events)
- 5 contractor classes with party buffs and ultimates
- Contract system (magic stones)
- Boss rush mode, New Game+

### Combat
- 346 skills with 11 mechanics
- Auto-battle, battle log, speed control
- Type effectiveness preview
- Floating damage numbers, skill particle effects
- Status effect animated icons
- Party buff system (5 classes)

### World
- 31 tile-based maps with Kenney RPG Base assets
- Weather effects (snow, fireflies, cave drips)
- Hidden event detection (magi sensing)
- NPC speech bubbles, idle animations
- Exit arrow indicators, water shimmer
- Minimap, encounter zone warnings

### Story
- 10 chapters with full voice/dialog
- 4 rival battles (scaling), 4 shadow clan battles
- 8 guardians + Elite Four gauntlet
- Multiple endings (Light/Dark/Neutral via karma)
- Post-game legendary encounters (8 legendaries)

### Quality of Life
- 3 difficulty settings
- Achievement system (15 milestones)
- Play statistics tracking
- Auto-save on town entry
- Monster nickname editing
- Battle item target selection
- Dex with habitat info
- Mobile touch controls
- PWA + favicon
